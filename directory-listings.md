## Notes

- BBB accreditation typically requires an application and sometimes a fee — check their current process before assuming a free listing covers everything.
- Angi listings often start as a "claim your business" profile that's free, with paid lead-generation tiers available later — the free profile alone is worth having for the citation/backlink value.
- If a platform asks for a physical address and you operate as a mobile/service-area business without a public storefront, most of these platforms support marking the listing as a "service area business" — use that option rather than leaving the address blank or entering a fake one.
