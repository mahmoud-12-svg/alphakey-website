Common Questions

Charlotte Locksmith FAQ

💵
How much does a locksmith cost in Charlotte, NC?

Pricing depends on the service. Standard lockouts in Charlotte typically start around $75, while rekeying and lock changes are priced per lock. We always confirm the exact price with you before any work begins — no surprises.

🚗
Can you unlock my car without damaging it?

Yes. Our technicians use professional automotive tools designed to open vehicles without damage to the door, lock, or paint. We handle car lockouts for most makes and models across Charlotte.

📍
What areas of Charlotte do you serve?

All of Charlotte — including Uptown, University City, Ballantyne, South End, NoDa, and Steele Creek — plus surrounding cities within 30 miles like Matthews, Pineville, Huntersville, Concord, and Gastonia.

🪪
What do I need to show for a home lockout?

For your security, we verify that you live at the property before opening it. A photo ID with the address, or ID plus proof of residence such as a utility bill or lease, is usually enough.

🔑
Is rekeying cheaper than replacing my locks?

In most cases, yes. Rekeying keeps your existing hardware and changes only the internal pins so old keys stop working. It's usually faster and more affordable than full replacement — a smart move after moving into a new home.

🕐
Are you open on Sunday?

We're open Monday through Saturday, 7AM to 10PM, and closed on Sundays. For lockouts and urgent lock issues during working hours, call (480) 339-0270 and we'll get help to you fast.
