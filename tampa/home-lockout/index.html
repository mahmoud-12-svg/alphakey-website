Home Lockout FAQ

💵
How much does a home lockout cost in Tampa, FL?

Standard home lockouts in Tampa typically start around $75. We confirm the exact price with you before any work begins.

🪪
What do I need to show for a home lockout?

For your security, we verify that you live at the property before opening it. A photo ID with the address, or ID plus proof of residence, is usually enough.

🚪
Will you have to drill or damage my door?

In most cases, no. We use non-destructive entry methods first and only consider drilling as a last resort, always discussed with you first.
