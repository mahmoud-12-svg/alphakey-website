Lock Rekeying Service

Same Locks, New Keys.

Rekeying keeps your existing hardware in place and changes the internal pins so old keys stop working and new ones are cut to match. It's our most common recommendation after a move, a lost key, or a tenant change. Common Questions

Lock Rekeying FAQ

💵
How much does rekeying cost in Tampa, FL?

Rekeying is priced per lock, typically $20 to $50 per cylinder for standard hardware, confirmed before we start.

🔑
Is rekeying cheaper than replacing my locks?

In most cases, yes. Rekeying keeps your existing hardware and changes only the internal pins, which is usually faster and more affordable than full replacement.

🏠
When should I rekey my locks?

Common times are after moving into a new home, after losing a key, or after a roommate or tenant moves out.
