Common Questions

Tampa Locksmith FAQ

💵
How much does a locksmith cost in Tampa, FL?

Pricing depends on the service. Standard lockouts in Tampa typically start around $75, while rekeying and lock changes are priced per lock. We always confirm the exact price with you before any work begins — no surprises.

🚗
Can you unlock my car without damaging it?

Yes. Our technicians use professional automotive tools designed to open vehicles without damage to the door, lock, or paint. We handle car lockouts for most makes and models across Tampa.

📍
What areas of Tampa do you serve?

All of Tampa — including Downtown, Ybor City, Hyde Park, Seminole Heights, Westshore, Carrollwood, and New Tampa — plus surrounding areas like Brandon, Riverview, Lutz, Wesley Chapel, and Temple Terrace.

🪪
What do I need to show for a home lockout?

For your security, we verify that you live at the property before opening it. A photo ID with the address, or ID plus proof of residence such as a utility bill or lease, is usually enough.

🔑
Is rekeying cheaper than replacing my locks?

In most cases, yes. Rekeying keeps your existing hardware and changes only the internal pins so old keys stop working. It's usually faster and more affordable than full replacement — a smart move after moving into a new home.

🕐
Are you open on Sunday?

We're open Monday through Saturday, 7AM to 10PM, and closed on Sundays. For lockouts and urgent lock issues during working hours, call (813) 498-3375 and we'll get help to you fast.
